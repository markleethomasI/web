Frequently Asked Questions

When should I consider replacing my fence?

Don’t wait for your fence to collapse. Years of weather exposure and potential wood rot can impact not just your fence’s appearance but also its structural integrity.

Why not get a Free Fence Quote and gain a second pair of eyes from our fence contractor experts? They can advise whether a repair or replacement is in order, and offer insight into the affordability of a new fence.

Do you have financing options?

Absolutely. We provide a range of financing solutions to fit almost every budget, making a new fence accessible for everyone.

When can you start and finish my fencing project?

We’re proud to handle numerous fencing projects every year. Start times can vary due to factors like weather, unforeseen global events, or shipping delays.

Despite these variables, we’re dedicated to completing your project promptly without compromising on quality. Our aim? Making every client feel like our top priority.
