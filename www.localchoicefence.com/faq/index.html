Fence Installation FAQ's

Installation & Service Areas

Do you offer fence installation in all counties in Florida?

We predominantly serve in Hillsborough, Sumter, Pasco, Lake County, Polk County, and Pasco County. But feel free to reach out; we’re always looking to assist more communities!

How long does a typical fence installation take?

The duration can vary based on the fence type and property size. However, our team is efficient, ensuring timely completion without compromising on quality.

Typically, smaller projects can be completed in as little as 1-3 days.

Do you offer commercial fence installations?

Yes! We provide both residential and commercial fence installations – including HOA communities. Whether it’s a cozy home or a sprawling business facility, we’ve got you covered.

Materials & Quality

What materials do you use for fencing?

We specialize in a wide range of fence styles, including:

  • Warm Traditional Wood
  • Low Maintenance Vinyl
  • Elegant Aluminum
  • Secure Chain Link
  • Countryside Ranch Rail
  • Sturdy Cross Buck
  • Wood and Wire Blend
  • Versatile Field Fences
  • Protective Barbed Wire

Why do you use screws for hand-built fences?

Screws provide a more robust and enduring grip. By using screws in our hand-built fences, we ensure they last longer and stand up better against the elements, offering superior longevity.

What makes your materials "sturdier" than competitors?

Our commitment to quality means we choose high-grade materials that undergo specific treatments and preparations. This enhances their durability, especially considering Florida’s challenging climate.

Design & Customization

Can I customize the design of my fence?

Absolutely! We believe every property is unique, and your fence should mirror that distinctiveness. Share your vision, and we’ll work together to bring it to life.

What's the difference between "paneled" and "hand-built" fences?

Paneled fences are pre-made sections installed sequentially. In contrast, hand-built fences are constructed on-site, allowing more adaptability and customization.

How do I decide which material or design is right for my property?

Our team of experts will guide you. Depending on your needs – whether it’s privacy, aesthetics, or both – we’ll suggest options that resonate with your vision and property.
